How to use MyCoHeB

1. Pan the map to select your area of interest (to zoom in or zoom out, use the + or . tools located at the left side of the map)
OR
Enter a city name to center the map on your area of interest using the search box which is located at the top left side of this page
2. Then, use rectangle or circle selection tools (located on the lower left hand corner of the map section) to precisely draw on the map your area of interest.
3. Click on 'Draw Chart' to have results returned here.
4. Share the results (email) with a friend or colleague by using the data sharing form to the right. You can also download the entire results in CSV format.

5. For more details on this click the next button below.
Tested browsers (all features tested and working): Internet Explorer 9, Opera 11, Sarfari 5, FireFox 12, Chrome 19
Browers with issues : Internet Explorer 8 search disabled, Internet Explorer 7 and lower have page render issues and search is disabled
